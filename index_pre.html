Hawdy.

My name is Xi Fang and I am a PhD student at the biomedical engineering department of Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. Before coming to RPI, I obtained Bachelor’s degree in Information Security from the School of Computing at Wuhan University. During my undergraduate period, I conducted two internships and got accessed to lots of cutting-edge technologies and state-of-the-art theories relevant to medical imaging and image processing. I am research assistant in Deep Imaging Analysitc Lab. My research focus is medical image processing, which includes feature recognition, detection and segmentation.

Researches.

My main research interest lies in the intersection of deep learning and medical image analysis. On one hand, I develop new deep learning models to tackle different machine learning problems. On the other hand, using deep learning techniques, I try to tackle some potential problems in medical images.

I also conducts research on various applications of deep learning. I believe that my research will assist medical professionals and help to achieve more intelligent diagnosis and treatment in the future.
